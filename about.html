About Us

Our mission has always been to find the best coffee from around the world and show everyone exactly how great specialty coffee can be. We search the world so you don’t have to.
A passion for coffee that has been cared for, and is loved from the moment it’s planted to the moment it’s brewed, is why we exist.
Our coffee experts travel the world sourcing, tasting and grading to select only the finest speciality coffee directly from the grower.
This way, we control all the variables every step of the way to make sure the coffee we deliver is perfect.
We try to select the world’s best locations for our single origins, and wherever possible we source this coffee direct trade. Our search for the best coffee doesn’t stop at finding the best tasting coffee.
we want to ensure we are responsible for positive action at every step of the coffee supply chain.
